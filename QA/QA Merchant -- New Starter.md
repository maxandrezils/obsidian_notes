## New starter

Hello and welcome to takealot!

These are the rounds of onboarding:

1.  The engineering onboarding
    1.  You should have received an excel template to go through. If not please speak to your team lead. This will help you setup up your laptop, VPN  access etc
    2.  [Sample doc](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1jb-rB1mS5Mr_Z2aUJSby_XlqUm99LWJTmC_mfsfJ1dg/edit#gid=1302822348)
2.  The QA team general onboarding: [New Starter](https://jira.takealot.com/wiki/display/TST/New+Starter)
3.   Team on-boarding  (Below)
